Meet our Duckies!

Mathias was a creature living in the Great Green Forest. One day, he was blind and awoke within a strange box of sorts Within the box, Mathias was surrounded by other fluffy friends. Some who were bigger than him and some who were smaller.

After a while, Mathias was finally freed from the darkness within the box. One by one, Mathias and his fluffy friends were picked up by person's hand. They were each placed on cold shelves on which they spent the night. When morning came, the place was surrounded by drunk adults and loud children.

In the same Dave & Buster's, Jeffery and his little brother Matthew were playing games for points. Luckily, Matthew pulled down on the handle to spin a wheel and won the jackpot, scoring them 1000 points! After spending all their credits, the brothers walked into the rewards shop. Having looked around for a while, Matthew picked up Mathias and brought him to the counter.

Mathias soon became best friends with Jeffery and Matthew and followed them whenever they went. Mathias came to school on the Ducky Meet and Greet and met Henry and e, who he quickly became friends with as they assisted in Jeffery and Henry's coding sessions
